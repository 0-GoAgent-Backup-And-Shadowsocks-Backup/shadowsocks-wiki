- aes-256-cfb: Default and recommended.
- aes-128-cfb
- aes-192-cfb
- bf-cfb
- camellia-128-cfb
- camellia-192-cfb
- camellia-256-cfb
- cast5-cfb
- des-cfb
- idea-cfb
- rc2-cfb
- seed-cfb
- salsa20-ctr

These methods are legacy and not safe. Do not use them.
